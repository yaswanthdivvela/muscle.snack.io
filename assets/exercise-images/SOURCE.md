Exercise-card images in this folder were composed locally from downloaded internet exercise references.

Most cards use same-style Spotebi exercise-guide illustrations. Pull-up-bar cards and the standing hip-circle card use paired photos from `yuhonas/free-exercise-db`, a few mobility/regression cards use direct references, and the foot-control cards use downloaded toe-yoga / short-foot reference visuals instead of project-local diagrams. The per-card source mapping and `matchQuality` (`exact`, `close`, or `bad`) are in `manifest.json`; `close` images must be treated as visual references and checked against the card cues.

Exercise cards identify engaged muscles with labeled chips only; the app does not render an anatomy overlay or approximate muscle-position markers.
