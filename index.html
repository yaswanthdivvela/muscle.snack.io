Safety note

This supports strength, range, tissue tolerance, and control for combat-sport movement; it does not teach punching, kicking, elbow, knee, clinch, takedown, defense, sparring, or tactical technique. Keep tendon discomfort at or below 3/10, smooth, and back to baseline within 24 hours. Stop for sharp pain, radiating pain, numbness, swelling, warmth, sudden weakness, or pain that changes movement; regress if symptoms worsen later or tomorrow.
